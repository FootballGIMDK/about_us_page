How do we use music?

Music is an international language – you don’t need to learn it in order to understand it, and it connects with you on a deep, emotional level, triggering responses you don’t even have to think about. And that’s why when it comes to working with your tastes and preferences, evterything we do starts with music. It’s the purest and most accurate reflection of what you like and – therefore – what you are like. We can even give song writers and producers unique insights into their music that can help them reach out to new, receptive audiences.
